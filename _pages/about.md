I am a MSc student in Economics at [Heidelberg University](https://www.awi.uni-heidelberg.de/en). I hold a BSc in Economics from the [University of Costa Rica](https://economia.ucr.ac.cr/), where I served as teaching assistant and research assistant at the [Economic Sciences Research Institute (IICE)](https://iice.ucr.ac.cr/).

My research interests include macroeconomics, monetary policy, and econometrics. You can find my CV [here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1V3e0Lt_hMumittRpfDoBhkLnTM5cuDsC/view?usp=sharing).
